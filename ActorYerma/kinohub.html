Yermakhan Malikuly Khamitov was born on September 1, 2007, in Semey, Kazakhstan. As the second child and first son, he showed an early interest in cinema, which led him to pursue acting.

His breakout role was in the superhero film "SuperErma," which defined his career and brought him global recognition.

He has acted in "Project X," "Justice League," and "Hacksaw Ridge," showing versatility in various roles.

Today, Yermakhan continues to build his career while remaining most known for the character of SuperErma.
